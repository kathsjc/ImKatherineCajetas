Are your services available to home owners?

No. Our highly standardized process, including fixed material selections and floor plans, is optimized solely for investors.

Do you build custom homes?

No. We do provide pre-development and design/build services to build-to-rent investors.

Do you do ADUs or home remodels?

No. We only build new ground up construction. We do not do home additions, attached ADUs, or remodel projects. We design and build detached accessory dwelling units (DADUs) on existing residential properties.

Do you work outside of Washington state?

Not at this time.
